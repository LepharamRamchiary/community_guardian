Our Story

Community Guardian was born from a simple observation: too many community issues go unnoticed or unaddressed because there's no easy way for residents to report them and track their resolution. We saw neighbors frustrated by potholes that never got fixed, streetlights that stayed broken for months, and community concerns that seemed to disappear into bureaucratic black holes.

I created Community Guardian as a learning project to better understand how to build platforms that simplify issue reporting and promote transparency in their resolution.

Today, I am provided with the chance to serve communities across the region, helping thousands of residents make their voices heard and create lasting positive change in neighborhoods everywhere.
